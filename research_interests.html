Current Research Interests
Below are some of the areas and questions that I am interested in. More information can be found by looking at my publications.
Superconducting proximity effect in topological insulators
  • Proximity effect in topologically protected surface states
  • Inverse effects in the superconductor from proximity to topological materials
Quantum information of topological materials
  • Entanglement entropy and fidelity of topological systems
  • Topological phase transitions
  • Dynamical phase transitions
Topological insulators and superconductors
  • Kink states in bilayer graphene
  • Majorana bound states in one and two dimensional systems
Interacting one dimensional physics and inhomogeneous systems
  • Luttinger liquids in inhomogeneous systems and traps
  • Carbon nanotubes
  • Transport and thermal properties
  • Multi-band wires
From non-Hermitian dynamics to thermalization
  • Dynamics of non-Hermitian systems
  • Thermalization and dynamics in closed and open quantum systems
Domain wall interactions and motion
  • Domain wall motion in wires
  • Electron mediated domain wall interactions
  • Ordering of domain walls
