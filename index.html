Welcome! I am a theoretical physicist working in the area of condensed matter physics. Of particular interest to me are low dimensional structures, topological and strongly correlated electron systems, and questions of dynamics and thermalization in quantum mechanics. You can find out more about it here, or by taking a look at my publications or CV, and you can contact me at nick@sedlmayr.co.uk.

I also write a blog on condensed matter physics and quantum physics called condensed matters.
