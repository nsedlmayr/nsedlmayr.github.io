Condensed Matter Coffee Meetings
The Condensed Matter Coffee Meetings take place every Tuesday at 1pm in the KFT coffee room.
Everyone is welcome, but all attendees must join the rotating pool of presenters!
Each week we will simply discuss whatever that weeks presenter wishes, whether a new interesting paper, their own work, a tricky integral, a subtle physicla conbcept or... perhaps simply the latest gossip.
